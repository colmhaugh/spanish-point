Surfing

Surfing information. Surfing began in Spanish Point in the late 1960's when travelling surfers from the UK and America came here to explore. Many left equipment behind and so the locals began to experiment. ... The beach at Spanish Point is ideal for beginners while the reefs to the north cater for the needs of the more advanced.
